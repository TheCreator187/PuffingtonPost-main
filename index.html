Elephants 41 by Elephants Growth

I've been able to get my hands on this strain only 3 times last year and yup it was very much worth it. We're not gonna talk about the price lol but being from NYC I really can't complain, its not easy finding this strain out here, but I can say this is the best weed I've ever smoked.

Elephant 41 has become a sensation in the cannabis community, gaining widespread attention after a viral tweet highlighted its unique qualities. While its exact genetics are unconfirmed, many believe it stems from Growth’s LCG cut, which may explain its standout attributes and premium smoking experience. The strain’s aroma is described as a mesmerizing mix of musky, funky, tropical pineapple, and sweet cherry notes, both delicate and vibrant. Users report a creamy smoothness on the inhale with tropical, musky undertones, while the exhale offers a rich, funky flavor with lasting muskiness.

Praised for its unparalleled smoothness, Elephants 41 is often compared to Mount Burnin’s Silky series. The strain is said to deliver a clean and balanced high, beginning with a swift cerebral uplift immediately after exhaling, followed by a relaxing body high that develops shortly after. This harmonious blend of effects makes it ideal for both casual users and experienced cannabis enthusiasts, offering an enjoyable experience suitable for any time of day. With its captivating aroma, luxurious texture, and versatile effects, Elephants 41 is quickly establishing itself as a standout in the cannabis world... TheCreator187


Silky Gelato by Mount Burnin

Silky Gelato, cultivated by Mount Burnin and distributed by Ted’s Budz, is a hybrid strain with an undisclosed lineage, though it is recognized as a variant of the Gelato family. Aroma and Flavor Profile: • Aroma: This strain presents a complex scent profile, featuring notes reminiscent of black permanent marker, coffee beans, oily sour funk, musky floor cleaner, and a core of hard cheese with black pepper horseradish. • Flavor: When smoked, users may detect flavors of sour fuel, vanilla dough, and a sugarcane finish. Vaporization can reveal grassy musk, herbal gumdrop, and a hint of lingering menthol.

Physical Characteristics: The buds are typically medium to small in size, with a mix of wide and fluffy popcorn nugs. They exhibit good humidity, with a satisfactory cure and trim. Effects: Silky Gelato is known for its balanced head and body high, offering relaxing and sedative effects that may lead to heavy limbs and eyelids. It is considered to have above-average strength. Availability: This strain has been available through various dispensaries, including Green Therapy NYC and Splash Bros., though availability may vary.
